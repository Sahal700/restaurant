ABOUT US

At Sushi hut, we bring the authentic flavors of Japan to Kochi. Since 2002, we've been dedicated to crafting exceptional dishes using the freshest ingredients. Led by Chef Shing, who trained in Tokyo, our menu features sushi, sashimi, ramen, and more, all prepared with meticulous care. Our serene, welcoming ambiance makes Sakura the perfect setting for any occasion. Join us and embark on a culinary journey that delights the senses.
